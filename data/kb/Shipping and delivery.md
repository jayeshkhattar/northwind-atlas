# Shipping & Delivery

## Processing time
Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed after 2pm ET ship
the next business day. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking once
your order ships.

## Delivery estimates
- Standard shipping: 4–7 business days (free on orders over $50)
- Expedited shipping: 2–3 business days ($12 flat)
- Overnight: next business day if ordered before 12pm ET ($25 flat)

We currently ship within the United States only. We do not ship to PO boxes for
items over 5 lbs, which includes espresso machines.

## Tracking your order
Once an order status shows "shipped", the tracking link in your confirmation
email becomes active within a few hours. If an order has been in "processing"
for more than 3 business days, contact support so we can investigate.

## Lost or delayed packages
If tracking shows no movement for 5+ business days, we'll open a carrier
investigation. Most delayed packages arrive within 48 hours of an investigation
being opened. If a package is confirmed lost, we ship a replacement at no cost.
