# Bean Subscription

## How it works
Subscribe to House Blend or Single Origin beans and get them delivered on a
schedule you choose: every 2, 4, or 6 weeks. Subscribers save 15% off the
one-time price on every delivery.

## Managing your subscription
You can change frequency, swap blends, skip a delivery, or pause anytime from
your account before the next billing date. Changes made after billing apply to
the following delivery.

## Billing
You're charged when each order ships, not in advance. The 15% discount is
applied automatically. Subscriptions renew until cancelled.

## Skipping vs pausing vs cancelling
- **Skip** — removes the next single delivery; the schedule resumes after.
- **Pause** — halts deliveries indefinitely until you resume.
- **Cancel** — ends the subscription entirely. No cancellation fee.

## Cancelling
Cancel anytime from your account or by contacting support. If you cancel after
an order has already been charged and shipped, that final shipment is not
refundable, but no further charges occur.

## Freshness guarantee
Beans ship within 5 days of roast. If your beans arrive past the roast window or
stale, contact support for a free replacement bag.
