# Warranty Policy

## Coverage by product
- Espresso Machine (ES-200): 2 years, parts and labor
- Burr Grinder (GR-100): 2 years, motor and burrs
- Pour-Over Kettle (KT-050): 1 year
- All other accessories: 1 year

## What's covered
Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship under normal household use.
This includes motor failure, electronic faults, and premature mechanical wear.

## What's not covered
- Normal wear (dulled burrs, worn gaskets after years of use)
- Damage from failure to maintain (scale buildup from not descaling)
- Accidental damage, drops, or liquid damage outside normal use
- Commercial use — all warranties assume household use only

## Making a warranty claim
Contact support with your order number and a description of the issue. For most
claims we'll ask for a photo or short video. Approved claims are resolved by
repair, replacement part, or full unit replacement at our discretion. Warranty
service does not extend the original warranty period.

## Proof of purchase
Your Northwind order number is sufficient proof of purchase. We can look it up
from the email on your account.
