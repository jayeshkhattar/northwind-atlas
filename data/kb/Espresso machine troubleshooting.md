# Espresso Machine (ES-200) Troubleshooting

## Error codes
The ES-200 displays an error code on the LED panel when it detects a fault:
- **E1** — Water tank empty or not seated. Refill and reseat the tank.
- **E2** — Overheating. Turn off and let the machine cool for 30 minutes.
- **E3** — Pump pressure fault. Usually scale buildup; run a descale cycle.
- **E4** — Internal sensor fault. This requires service; contact support.

## No water / weak flow
Most flow problems are scale. Run a descale cycle with Northwind descaling
solution (or food-grade citric acid). Hard-water areas should descale monthly.

## Espresso comes out too fast (watery)
The grind is too coarse or the dose is too low. Use a finer grind on your
GR-100 (a lower number) and aim for an 18g dose in the double basket.

## Espresso comes out too slow (or not at all)
The grind is too fine, choking the machine. Go coarser. If a descale hasn't been
done recently, scale may also be restricting flow.

## Machine leaks water underneath
Check the drip tray isn't overfull. If water pools after the tray is emptied,
the tank gasket may be worn — contact support for a replacement gasket.

## Warranty
The ES-200 carries a 2-year warranty. Damage from failure to descale is not
covered, so keep a record of your descaling.
