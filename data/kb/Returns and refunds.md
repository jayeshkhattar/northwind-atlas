# Returns & Refunds

## Return window
Unopened, unused items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full
refund. Opened items (including used grinders and machines) can be returned
within 14 days if they are in resalable condition.

## Items that cannot be returned
- Coffee beans, once opened (food safety)
- Water filters, once removed from sealed packaging
- Gift cards

## How refunds are calculated
Refunds are issued to the original payment method. The refund covers the item
price. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to
a defect or our error. For partial returns (some items in an order kept), the
refund is prorated to the returned items only.

## Refund timing
Once we receive and inspect a returned item, refunds are processed within 3–5
business days. It may take an additional 5–10 business days for the refund to
appear on your statement, depending on your bank.

## Defective items
If an item arrives defective or is damaged in transit, contact support within
7 days with a photo. We cover return shipping and send a replacement or full
refund including original shipping.
